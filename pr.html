CANADA

Welcome to Canada’s Permanent Residency Program!

AIHTISAL extends a warm welcome to individuals worldwide. Our Permanent Residency Program for Canada is meticulously crafted to offer skilled workers, investors, entrepreneurs, and their families the chance to establish Canada as their permanent home.

Embarking on the Permanent Residency Program journey may seem intricate, but fear not. Our team of seasoned Migration consultants is dedicated to assisting you. We'll navigate you through the process, ensuring all requirements are met, and your application is thorough and precise. Your Canadian permanent residency awaits – let us guide you toward a new chapter in your life.

  • BENEFITS
    As a permanent resident of Canada, you will have access to a wide range of benefits, including:
    • Healthcare: Permanent residents of Canada have access to free basic healthcare services provided by the government.
    • Education: Permanent residents of Canada can access Canada’s world-class education system at significantly lower tuition rates than international students.
    • Employment: Permanent residents of Canada have the right to work and live anywhere in the country, as well as the right to apply for Canadian citizenship.
    • Social Benefits: Permanent residents of Canada can access a variety of social benefits, including retirement and disability benefits, unemployment insurance, and child benefits.
    • Diversity and Inclusivity: Canada is known for its welcoming and inclusive society that celebrates diversity. As a permanent resident, you will have the opportunity to become a part of this vibrant and multicultural community.

  • PROGRAMS
    Our Canada Permanent Residency Program has different categories, each with its specific requirements and eligibility criteria. These categories include:
    • Express Entry: This category is for skilled workers who want to come to Canada as permanent residents. To be eligible, you must meet the requirements of one of the three federal economic immigration programs: the Federal Skilled Worker Program, the Federal Skilled Trades Program, or the Canadian Experience Class.
    • Provincial Nominee Program (PNP): This category allows Canadian provinces and territories to nominate candidates who meet their specific economic and labor market needs. Each province and territory has its own PNP program.
    • Family Sponsorship: This category allows Canadian citizens and permanent residents to sponsor their eligible family members to come to Canada as permanent residents.
    • Business Immigration: This category is for people who want to start or invest in a business in Canada. There are several programs under this category, including the Start-up Visa Program, the Self-employed Persons Program, and the Investor Program.

AUSTRALIA

Welcome to Australia’s Permanent Residency Program!

The Australia Permanent Residency Program can turn your dream into reality. As a permanent resident, you'll discover the country's breathtaking beauty, enjoy world-class education and healthcare, and experience a high standard of living in a diverse society. Our streamlined programs provide access to Australia's robust economy, offering numerous opportunities for both career and personal growth.

Navigating the complex permanent residency application process can be overwhelming, but our team of experienced migration agents is here to support you at every step. From eligibility assessment to visa processing, we are dedicated to helping you achieve your goal of becoming a permanent resident of Australia.

  • BENEFITS

    Benefits of Becoming a Permanent Resident of Australia

    • Work and Study: As a permanent resident, you have the right to work and study in Australia without any restrictions. You can also access government-funded education and training programs.
    • Health Care: Access Australia’s public health care system, Medicare, providing access to medical services and subsidized prescription medicines.
    • Social Security: Eligibility for social security benefits such as unemployment, sickness, and parenting payments.
    • Citizenship: After living in Australia for a certain period as a permanent resident, you may be eligible to apply for Australian citizenship.
    • Travel: As a permanent resident, you can travel in and out of Australia as many times as you like and apply for an Australian passport.
    • Family Sponsorship: Sponsor your family members to come and live in Australia once you become a permanent resident.
    • Quality of Life: Enjoy Australia's high standard of living, safe and friendly communities, and beautiful natural environment, enhancing the quality of life for you and your family.

  • PROGRAMS
    Australia Permanent Residency Program Options
    • Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189): This program is designed for skilled workers who have the qualifications and experience that are in demand in Australia. To be eligible, you must meet the points test requirements and have an occupation on the Skilled Occupation List (SOL).
    • Skilled Nominated Visa (Subclass 190): This program is similar to the Skilled Independent Visa, but requires a nomination from a state or territory government. If you have a particular state or territory in mind, this may be the best option for you.
    • Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme (Subclass 187): This program is designed to encourage skilled workers to live and work in regional areas of Australia. To be eligible, you must have an employer who is willing to sponsor you and meet the skills and qualifications requirements.
    • Business Talent (Permanent) Visa (Subclass 132): This program is designed for business owners, investors, and entrepreneurs who want to establish or develop a business in Australia. To be eligible, you must have a successful business career and be able to make a significant contribution to the Australian economy.
    • Partner Visa (Subclass 820/801): This program is for people who are in a genuine and ongoing relationship with an Australian citizen or permanent resident. To be eligible, you must meet certain criteria and provide evidence of your relationship.

NEW ZEALAND

Welcome to New Zealand Permanent Residency Program!

The New Zealand Permanent Residency Program opens doors to turn your aspirations into reality. As a permanent resident, you will uncover the mesmerizing beauty of the country, delight in world-class education and healthcare, and immerse yourself in a high standard of living within a vibrant and diverse society. Our efficient programs grant access to New Zealand's thriving economy, providing abundant opportunities for professional advancement and personal development.

Navigating the intricacies of the permanent residency application process can be daunting, but rest assured, our team of seasoned migration experts is here to guide you at every phase. From initial eligibility assessments to the completion of visa processes, we are committed to assisting you in realizing your objective of becoming a permanent resident of New Zealand.

  • BENEFITS
    Benefits of New Zealand Permanent Residency
    • Work and Study Opportunities: Access to work and study opportunities without restrictions.
    • Public Healthcare Access: Eligibility for New Zealand's public healthcare system, ensuring access to medical services and subsidized prescription medications.
    • Social Security Benefits: Availability of social security benefits, including unemployment support and other welfare programs.
    • Citizenship Pathway: Opportunity to apply for New Zealand citizenship after meeting residency requirements.
    • Travel Flexibility: Freedom to travel in and out of New Zealand without constraints, providing ease of mobility.
    • Family Reunification: Option to sponsor eligible family members for permanent residency, promoting family reunification.
    • Quality of Life: Enjoyment of New Zealand's high standard of living, safe communities, and stunning natural landscapes.
    • Diverse and Inclusive Society: Integration into a diverse and inclusive society that embraces cultural differences.
    • Education Access: Access to New Zealand's world-class education system, often at favorable rates for permanent residents.
    • Business and Investment Opportunities: Opportunities for business ownership, entrepreneurship, and investment in New Zealand.

  • PROGRAMS
    New Zealand Permanent Residency Programs
    • Skilled Migrant Category (SMC): For skilled workers with qualifications and experience in demand in New Zealand. Points-based system, considering factors like age, work experience, and qualifications.
    • Residence from Work (RFW): Designed for temporary work visa holders who have been working in New Zealand and wish to transition to permanent residency.
    • Investor Program: Allows individuals with significant funds for investment in New Zealand to apply for PR. Different investment thresholds and criteria may apply.
    • Entrepreneur Work Visa: For individuals with a successful track record in business who want to establish or buy a business in New Zealand. Can lead to PR.
    • Family Categories: Allows family members of New Zealand citizens or PR holders to apply for PR. Categories may include partners, dependent children, and parents.
    • Parent Retirement Category: Provides a pathway for parents to join their adult children in New Zealand if they meet specific financial and health criteria.
    • Refugee Family Support Category: For refugees settled in New Zealand, this program enables them to sponsor their family members to join them.
    • Pacific Access Category: Provides a limited number of places each year for citizens of certain Pacific Island nations to apply for PR.
    • Samoa Quota Scheme: A specific program for citizens of Samoa to apply for PR under a quota system.
    • Talent (Accredited Employers) Work Visa: Targets highly skilled workers with a job offer from an accredited New Zealand employer. Can lead to PR.

Welcome to SCHENGEN Permanent Residency Program!

The Schengen Permanent Residency Program opens avenues for transforming your dreams into reality. As a permanent resident, you'll have the opportunity to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the Schengen Area, enjoy top-notch education and healthcare facilities, and savor a high standard of living in this diverse and interconnected region.

The Schengen Permanent Residency Program opens avenues for transforming your dreams into reality. As a permanent resident, you'll have the opportunity to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the Schengen Area, enjoy top-notch education and healthcare facilities, and savor a high standard of living in this diverse and interconnected region. Our streamlined programs provide access to the robust economies of the Schengen countries, offering numerous prospects for professional growth and personal development.

  • BENEFITS
    Schengen Permanent Residency Benefits
    • Freedom of Movement: Unrestricted travel within the Schengen countries, allowing for seamless movement across borders without internal border controls.
    • Residence Flexibility: Ability to reside in any of the Schengen countries, providing flexibility in choosing a place of residence based on personal preferences.
    • Work Opportunities: Access to employment opportunities in any Schengen country without the need for additional work permits.
    • Business and Investment: Facilitation of business and investment activities across Schengen countries, promoting economic engagement.
    • Education Access: Eligibility to enroll in educational institutions within the Schengen Area, often at favorable conditions for permanent residents.
    • Healthcare Benefits: Access to healthcare services in any Schengen country, ensuring comprehensive medical care when needed.
    • Social Security Coverage: Eligibility for social security benefits, including retirement and disability benefits, unemployment support, and family-related benefits.
    • Family Reunification: Possibility to sponsor eligible family members for permanent residency, promoting family reunification in the Schengen Area.
    • Cultural Integration: Integration into a diverse cultural environment, fostering cultural exchange and understanding within the Schengen community.
    • Pathway to Citizenship: In some Schengen countries, PR may serve as a pathway to citizenship, offering a long-term connection to the local community.

  • PROGRAMS
    Schengen Permanent Residency Programs
    • Long-Term Residency Permit: Many Schengen countries offer long-term residency permits for individuals who have been living in the country for an extended period.
    • Entrepreneur/Investor Visa: Residency programs for entrepreneurs and investors contributing to the country's economy through business activities.
    • Skilled Worker Programs: Programs for skilled workers with in-demand skills and qualifications.
    • Family Reunification: Residency granted to family members of existing residents or citizens.
    • Student Visa to PR: Transition from student visa to long-term residency for those who have completed their studies.
