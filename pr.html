CANADA

Welcome to Canada’s Permanent Residency Program!

AIHTISAL extends a warm welcome to individuals worldwide. Our Permanent Residency Program for Canada is meticulously crafted to offer skilled workers, investors, entrepreneurs, and their families the chance to establish Canada as their permanent home.

Embarking on the Permanent Residency Program journey may seem intricate, but fear not. Our team of seasoned immigration consultants is dedicated to assisting you. We'll navigate you through the process, ensuring all requirements are met, and your application is thorough and precise. Your Canadian permanent residency awaits – let us guide you toward a new chapter in your life.

  • BENEFITS

    As a permanent resident of Canada, you will have access to a wide range of benefits, including:

    • Healthcare: Permanent residents of Canada have access to free basic healthcare services provided by the government.
    • Education: Permanent residents of Canada can access Canada’s world-class education system at significantly lower tuition rates than international students.
    • Employment: Permanent residents of Canada have the right to work and live anywhere in the country, as well as the right to apply for Canadian citizenship.
    • Social benefits: Permanent residents of Canada can access a variety of social benefits, including retirement and disability benefits, unemployment insurance, and child benefits.
    • Diversity and inclusivity: Canada is known for its welcoming and inclusive society that celebrates diversity. As a permanent resident, you will have the opportunity to become a part of this vibrant and multicultural community.

  • PROGRAMS

    Our Canada Permanent Residency Program has different categories, each with its specific requirements and eligibility criteria. These categories include:

    • Express Entry: This category is for skilled workers who want to come to Canada as permanent residents. To be eligible, you must meet the requirements of one of the three federal economic immigration programs: the Federal Skilled Worker Program, the Federal Skilled Trades Program, or the Canadian Experience Class.
    • Provincial Nominee Program (PNP): This category allows Canadian provinces and territories to nominate candidates who meet their specific economic and labor market needs. Each province and territory has its own PNP program.
    • Family Sponsorship: This category allows Canadian citizens and permanent residents to sponsor their eligible family members to come to Canada as permanent residents.
    • Business Immigration: This category is for people who want to start or invest in a business in Canada. There are several programs under this category, including the Start-up Visa Program, the Self-employed Persons Program, and the Investor Program.
